How to Use ReviewBoost

ReviewBoost helps you manage, request, and respond to customer reviews across Google, Yelp, and Facebook — all from one beautiful dashboard.

Dashboard

Your at-a-glance overview of review performance across all platforms.

1

Check your Average Rating, Total Reviews, and Auto-Posted stat cards.

2

Monitor your Response Rate — aim for 90%+.

3

Click "Respond" on recent reviews to reply directly.

InvoiceReady Integration

Automatically request reviews when invoices are paid.

1

Go to Settings → InvoiceReady Integration to configure the connection.

2

Incoming review requests appear in Request Reviews → From InvoiceReady.

3

Click "Preview Request" to see what your customer experiences.

4

Submitted reviews are auto-posted to all connected platforms.
