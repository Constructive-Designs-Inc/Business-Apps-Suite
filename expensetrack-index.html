How to Use ExpenseTrack

Your complete guide

ExpenseTrack helps you log, categorize, and analyze your business expenses in one place. Track spending by category, set monthly budgets, monitor tax-deductible expenses, scan receipts, manage recurring costs, connect your bank via Plaid, and generate reports — all from a clean, intuitive interface.

Your at-a-glance overview of spending, budgets, bank reconciliation, and trends.

A searchable, sortable table of every expense. Receipt-scanned items are locked for accuracy.

Scan physical receipts to automatically extract line items. Images are stored for IRS retention.

Manage auto-recurring expenses. Active items are auto-posted when due.

Connect your bank via Plaid to import transactions and reconcile spending.

Set and monitor monthly budgets — both overall and per-category.

Generate detailed spending summaries, category breakdowns, trends, and tax reports.

Tips & Tricks

Tax time saver: Use the Reports tab's Tax Deductible Summary and export to CSV right before filing.

Bank reconciliation: Connect via Plaid in the Bank tab for automatic alignment between bank and tracked expenses.

Recurring expenses: Set them up once in the Recurring tab — they auto-post to your expenses when due.
